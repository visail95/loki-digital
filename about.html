ABOUT US

25+ Years of Experience in Information Communication Technology

Company A is a 100% bumiputra company focusing in Information Communication Technology Solution. The company was incorporated on the 20th of August 1996 and since then, Company A has grown rapidly from a start-up company into a well-diversified business committed to secure the vast expanding IT requirement. At Company A, we understand the need for sustainable behavior and work towards this by applying well-defined values to all aspects of our daily business.
The company endeavors to be a profitable market driven business dedicated to supporting the government’s drives towards the enhancement of IT reconstruction and development. From modest beginning, the company has made great strides in the domain of multimedia presentation solutions and computerizes electronic identification solution that includes security, access card, smart building, software development and networking.
As a corollary, we are constantly on the lookout for promising technologies that support our customer needs. The source of our strength is in our team of dedicated experience management director, system engineers, project manager, consultant, technical support as well as our administration and finance personnel.
